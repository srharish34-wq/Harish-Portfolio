From a College Desk to a Real Business

The story of how HR Web Solutions came to life

HR Web Solutions was founded by me — Harish SR — during my 2nd year of college, born not from a business plan but from a burning desire to build things that actually matter. As CEO & Founder, I lead a dedicated team of freelance professionals, taking full ownership of every project — from the first client call to the final deployment. What started as a small experiment quickly evolved into a structured micro-agency, trusted by multiple clients to deliver high-quality, result-driven digital solutions across a wide range of services.

Together, we have successfully completed over 15 to 20 projects spanning full-stack web development, PHP & CRM systems, AI-powered applications, e-commerce platforms, mobile apps, and UI/UX design. Every project has been a classroom — sharpening our skills, deepening our understanding of real client needs, and teaching us what it truly means to deliver under pressure. This venture has given me more than experience — it has given me the confidence, discipline, and professional mindset to build solutions that go beyond code.
